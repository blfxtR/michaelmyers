= ++++                             About                                    ++++=
=================================================================================

	Michael Myers is a mod in which a Serial Killer called Michael Myers hunts 
	the other Team, the Hiders. He is a bit faster and has more Health than the 
	Hiders. If he found all Hiders except one the last Hider has the Chance to 
	kill Michael with a Knife. But always keep silent and patient.

	Are you strong enough to fight against a real Serial Killer?
